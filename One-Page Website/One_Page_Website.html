The Hawkins Middle School Av Club


Do you have a passion for telling stories? Are you interested in learning about television, film, and radio production?


The Audio-Visual (AV) Club at Hawkins Middle School is an interactive production experience, combining industry standard software, equipment, and techniques. The goal of the AV Club is to expand knowledge and practice skills related to filming, editing, and enhancing self-directed projects.


Club Members:

  • President: Dustin Henderson
  • Vice President: Mike Wheeler
  • Secretary: Lucas Sinclair
  • Treasurer: Will Byers
  • Faculty Advisor: Mr. Clark

The AV Club meets Tuesdays and Thursdays after school in Mr. Clark's classroom.

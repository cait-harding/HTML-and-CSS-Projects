About Us:

Located in the heart of downtown Boston, Academy Cinemas offers multiple screens of non-stop, world-class entertainment, from new releases to cinema classics.

Our auditoriums are equipped with Christie 4K RGB laser projection and Dolby Atmos sound, as well as 100% luxury recliner seating.

With a full bar and specialty food menu, Academy Cinemas is the perfect venue to enjoy time with family and friends! ♿
